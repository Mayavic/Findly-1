Under the hood

An orchestrated army of AI agents

Findly isn't a simple filter: it's a system of autonomous AI agents curating the fashion web end to end.

Autonomous exploration agents

Each agent owns a set of stores, browses their catalogs and extracts the relevant products — in parallel, in real time.

Semantic understanding

Your sentence and every product become vectors. Agents compare meaning, not keywords — so results are right even without an exact term.

Matching & deduplication

Agents recognize that the same model sold across 6 sites is one product, so you compare truly comparable prices.

Curation & multi-criteria ranking

A synthesis agent ranks everything by your intent: price, relevance, seller trust, reviews and delivery combined into one score.

Our story

Two friends, one shared obsession

We met in business school, already redesigning the fashion world between two classes. We each went on to build our careers in luxury — packaging, brand image, an obsession with detail at every step.

That's when we realized something: even with all that expertise, finding THE right piece of clothing at the right price is still a real headache — even for us. So we decided to fix it, together. Findly is that conviction, launched today, by the two of us.

FAQ

The questions we get asked

Is Findly really free?

Yes. You never pay. When you buy via a Findly link, the store pays us a commission — without changing your price.

Which stores are covered?

Over 50 at launch (major retailers, DTC brands and second-hand like Vinted), and we add more every week.

How do the agents understand my request?

Through semantic search: your sentence is matched against the meaning of products, not exact keywords. Relevant results even when vague.

Do you sell my data?

No. Your email is only used to notify you at launch. EU hosting, GDPR compliant, one-click unsubscribe.

When does it launch?

The beta opens gradually. Join the waitlist for priority access.
